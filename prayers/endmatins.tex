\ourCommentary{Prayer from the Proper of the season} \\
Grant, we beseech thee, O Almighty God: that we who anticipate the resurrection of thy Son with pious expectation, may obtain the glory of the same resurrection.\\ \\
\ourCommentary{Finish silently}\\
Through the same Jesus Christ, thy Son, Our Lord, Who liveth and reigneth with thee in the unity of the Holy Ghost,\\
 God, world without end. \\ \\
